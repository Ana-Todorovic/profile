About Ana

I thrive in the dynamic realm of product management. When I discovered product management many years ago, all I could think about was how to become a PM. I spent what seemed like endless amounts of time researching and building up my knowledge on how to be the best PM. I remember fondly the day I got my first PM job offer. I couldn't have been more excited. Still to this day, I read countless books on product management so I don't miss out on any important new developments in the field. My approach involves aligning product roadmaps with overarching business objectives. By inspiring and leading with empathy, I foster a collaborative environment where cross-functional teams thrive to develop high-quality products on time and within budget.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my 2 dogs and I try to cook new recipes every chance I get. Reading has become an important pasttime where I have the opportunity to learn new things while also taking the time to relax and recharge. I thoroughly enjoy developing new skills, and I am constantly looking for things to learn. Some of my most recent acquired random skills are gem faceting, quilling, and paper making.
