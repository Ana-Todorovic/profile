Work Life

I spent countless hours researching and building up my knowledge on how to become the best PM. I remember fondly the day I got my first PM job offer. I couldn't have been more excited. Process
Ideation & requirements gathering

Brainstorm with other stakeholders and gather business requirements for possible features.

Research & data gathering

Research the market and look for internal data that supports features.

Prioritization

Features that have supporting data are prioritized early and often.

Define epics and user stories

The feaures with the highest business value are turned into epics and user stories.

UX/UI design

Work with designers to get wireframes and hi-fi designs created

Develop

Epics and user stories are refined with dev teams and they start build.

Launch

Once all stories are complete and throughly tested, features are launched!

QA

Track data after lauch, make improvements and keep an eye out for new feature opportunities. Repeat!
